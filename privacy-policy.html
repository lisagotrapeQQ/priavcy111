OceanBox Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 3, 2026

OceanBox is designed to keep user-selected vault content on the user's device. This policy describes the data handled by OceanBox and how that data is used, stored, shared, retained, and deleted.

2. Summary

  • OceanBox does not use advertising SDKs.
  • OceanBox does not use analytics SDKs.
  • OceanBox does not sell user data.
  • OceanBox does not create a server account for the user.
  • Vault photos, videos, and notes are stored locally on the user's device.
  • Vault content is encrypted before local storage.
  • User-initiated sharing sends selected content only to the destination chosen by the user through the system share interface.

5. How OceanBox Uses Data

OceanBox uses data only for app functionality selected or initiated by the user. These uses include:

  • Importing photos and videos into the vault.
  • Encrypting, storing, displaying, playing, exporting, deleting, and sharing vault content.
  • Saving and editing private notes.
  • Verifying the vault password or guest password.
  • Maintaining local preferences and setup state.
  • Applying user-selected Family Controls restrictions on supported iOS versions.

6. Sharing and Third Parties

OceanBox does not sell user data and does not share user data with advertising networks or analytics services.

When the user chooses to share a photo, video, or text through the system share interface, the selected content is provided to the destination chosen by the user. The privacy practices of that destination are controlled by the selected third-party app or service, not by OceanBox.

OceanBox uses Apple system frameworks and platform services, including Photos, Keychain, Family Controls, Managed Settings, and the iOS share interface, to provide app functionality.

7. Storage and Security

  • Vault photos, videos, and notes are encrypted using AES-GCM before being stored in the app's private local storage.
  • Encryption keys and password hashes are stored using platform secure storage where implemented.
  • OceanBox does not operate a backend server for storing vault content.
  • Data remains on the user's device unless the user exports it, shares it, saves it to the photo library, restores it through device-level backup behavior, or otherwise transfers it outside the app.

8. Retention and Deletion

Vault data is retained on the user's device until the user deletes it in OceanBox, removes OceanBox from the device, or deletes it through device or operating system controls.

Users can delete photos, videos, and notes inside OceanBox. If the user imported media from the photo library, deleting the vault copy does not necessarily delete the original photo library asset unless the user separately confirms deletion of the original asset where that option is offered.

Because OceanBox does not maintain a backend account system for vault content, deletion requests for local vault data must be completed on the user's device. Users may contact Ludo Bouet at the email address below for privacy questions.

9. Consent and User Control

Users control whether to grant or deny system permissions through iOS settings and system permission prompts. Users may revoke permissions in iOS Settings. Revoking a permission may prevent related features from working.

Users control whether to import, create, share, export, or delete vault content. Users control whether to enable or disable supported Family Controls functionality.

10. Children's Privacy

OceanBox is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children through a backend service. The app stores user-selected vault content locally on the device. If a parent or guardian has questions about local data stored in OceanBox, they may contact Ludo Bouet using the contact information below.

11. International Availability

OceanBox may be available in multiple regions. Users are responsible for using the app in compliance with local requirements that apply to their own use of vault, media, sharing, and device restriction features.

12. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated when OceanBox changes its data practices, app functionality, permissions, or third-party integrations. The effective date above will be updated when the policy changes.
